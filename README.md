### 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes *3,900+ retail transactions* to uncover customer behavior patterns such as product demand, spending behavior, discount usage, and subscription preferences.
Insights gained support *data-driven business decisions* in product marketing, customer segmentation, and revenue optimization.

---

### 🧹 Data Preparation

* Cleaned and standardized dataset for consistency
* Resolved missing values using category-based *median imputation*
* Engineered new features like *age_group* & *purchase_frequency_days*
* Exported cleaned dataset into PostgreSQL for scalable querying

---

### 🛢 SQL Analysis

Conducted analytical queries to answer key business questions:

* Revenue breakdown by *gender & age groups*
* Identified *loyal, new & returning customer* segments
* Top *5 high-rated & frequently purchased* products
* Discount impact on total spending
* Shipping type vs revenue contribution

---

### 📊 Power BI Dashboard 

Visualizations include:

* Customer loyalty distribution
* Product rating & demand insights
* Revenue performance across segments
* Discount-driven profitability analysis
### 📊 Power BI Dashboard Preview

This dashboard highlights key insights including customer segmentation, revenue distribution, subscription adoption, and buying patterns.

### 🔍 Key Insights

✔ 80%+ customers classified as *loyal buyers* → strengthen retention benefits
✔ *Express shipping* users contributed *higher revenue*
✔ Top-rated items aligned with *high order frequency* → product promotion strategy
✔ High discount users → review pricing to protect margins
